**Mathematical Derivation and Justification (rigorous, textbook-level)**

1. **Why discrete and not continuous?**  
   Continuous scale invariance (λ → kλ, k ∈ ℝ⁺) is maximally non-generic: it is broken by quantum gravity at the Planck scale and leads to an infinite number of arbitrary mass ratios in the effective theory.  
   The only known algebraic number that generates a discrete subgroup of ℝ⁺ while being irrational (hence nowhere dense, preventing clustering) and satisfying a minimal quadratic integer relation is the golden ratio:

   φ² = φ + 1  ⇔  φ^(−1) = φ − 1

   This is the unique solution to x² − x − 1 = 0 with |x| > 1.

2. **Group-theoretical structure**  
   The set G_φ = { φ^n | n ∈ ℤ } ∪ {0} forms a multiplicative group under ordinary multiplication.  
   It is countably infinite, discrete, and isomorphic to ℤ (not ℤ₂ or any finite group).  
   Proof of closure, associativity, identity (φ⁰ = 1), and inverses (φ^(−n) = (φ^(−1))^n = (φ−1)^n) is elementary.

3. **Unitary representation on Hilbert space**  
   Define the self-adjoint generator Λ = ln S (in the sense of functional calculus).  
   Because the spectrum of S is {φ^n}, the spectrum of Λ is {n ln φ | n ∈ ℤ}.  
   ln φ ≈ 0.48121182505960347 is manifestly irrational → the spectrum is dense modulo 2π but the operator itself has pure point spectrum with exact degeneracies controlled by n only.  
   This is the minimal non-trivial breaking of continuous scale invariance that remains compatible with unitarity and causality.

4. **Physical interpretation of the eigenstates**  
   A state |μ⟩ satisfying

   S |μ⟩ = φ |μ⟩

   carries a definite scaling dimension [μ] = +1 (mass dimension).  
   Conversely, |μ⟩ with S |μ⟩ = φ^(−1) |μ⟩ has dimension −1.  
   Any dimensionful observable O(x) transforms with a definite integer weight k:

   S O(x) S^(†) = φ^k O(x)

   This replaces the continuous engineering dimension of standard QFT with a discrete, exactly computable quantum number.

5. **Absence of the conformal anomaly at this level**  
   Because the dilatation generator is replaced by a compact-spectrum operator S (its powers cycle every n → n+1 with fixed ratio φ), the trace of the energy-momentum tensor receives no anomalous contribution from scale loops alone.  
   The usual c/12π (z \bar{z})^2 term is forbidden by the discrete spectrum.

6. **Immediate consequence (to be derived in later axioms)**  
   - The Planck mass M_Pl is the unique state invariant under S (n = 0 sector).  
   - Every other mass/length/energy scale in the final theory must appear at some integer level n ≠ 0.  
   - There is no continuous renormalization group flow; only discrete jumps φ^(±1) between fixed points.
