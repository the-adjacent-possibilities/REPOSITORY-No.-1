$\pi_1(L(3,1)) = \mathbb{Z}_3$ classifies chiral zero modes.

\textbf{Uniqueness theorem}
$N_{\text{generations}} = 3$ is the only integer solution to the $\mathbb{Z}_3^3$ discrete anomaly cancellation condition.
