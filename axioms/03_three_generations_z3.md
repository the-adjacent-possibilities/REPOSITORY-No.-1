# Axiom 3 — Exactly Three Generations from ℤ₃ Persistent Torsion

**Enunciation**
The vacuum $\mathcal{M}_{vac}$ contains a canonically embedded lens space $L(3,1) = S^3/\mathbb{Z}_3$ as a persistent 3-cycle.
$\pi_1(L(3,1)) = \mathbb{Z}_3$ classifies chiral zero modes.
Anomaly cancellation (gauge + gravitational + discrete) forces exactly three generations of Standard Model fermions.

**Topological invariants**
$$\pi_1(L(3,1)) = \mathbb{Z}_3, \quad [L(3,1)] \in H_3(\mathcal{M}_{vac}; \mathbb{Z})^{\text{persistent}}$$

**Uniqueness theorem**
$N_{\text{generations}} = 3$ is the only integer solution to
$$\sum_{i=1}^N q_i^k = 0 \pmod{3} \quad (k=1,2,3)$$
with $q_i \in \{1, \omega, \omega^2\}$ and one fermion per sector.
